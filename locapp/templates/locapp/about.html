All about Sayahat

Sayahat came from an Urdu word meaning Tourism so basically this is a tourism website that helps both local and international tourists find and explore beautiful and far-flung areas of Pakistan and to find new destinations. The main aim of Sayahat is to promote tourism in Pakistan and boost the economy of Pakistan by helping tourists all over the globe to explore the magnificent beauty of Pakistan. In Pakistan, there are several activities which can be a great source of rapture for globetrotters like paragliding, rock climbing, mountain climbing, skiing, trekking/hiking, mountain biking, hot air ballooning, rafting and much more. Sayahat is divided into two main halves one is related to adventure clubs and the other one is for solo tourists.

Adventure Clubs

If a tourist wants to join a community and travel with a clique of enthusiasts to delve into the marvelous beauty of Pakistan, he can join any event that is being held by the adventure clubs of Pakistan. This will not only provide a pool of eager adventure partners, but it will be the impetus for new beginnings and new friendships, as well as some great adventure stories. On the other hand, the adventure club owners can register their clubs and post the events that are going to start soon.

Solo Tourism

For a person who wants to travel alone or with his family members and friends, Sayahat provides amenity to him too. He can search for any of his desired spot and get complete data of that location along with downright guide to get there using shortest possible path. He can get the exact total distance from his current location to the searched destination. Further, he can get to know about the availability of the nearest hotels, restaurants, refilling pump stations etc. In this way exploring Pakistan becomes far easy than it was ever before.
